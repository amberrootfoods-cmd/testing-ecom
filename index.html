Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Popkaari

What is Popkaari Makhana? ▼

Popkaari is premium, handpicked raw makhana (fox nuts) from trusted farmers. Our makhana features XL grain size, is 100% natural, and has been selected for quality and crunch. You can roast it your way, add it to kheer, or mix it into your own creations.

Is it really 100% natural? ▼

Yes! Popkaari contains only raw makhana and natural flavors (for flavored variants). No artificial additives, preservatives, or colors. Just pure makhana as nature intended.

How do I cook Raw Makhana? ▼

Raw makhana is versatile! You can dry roast it in a pan over medium heat, add to kheer while cooking, or blend it into flours. It's perfect for vrat (fasting) too. Each serving offers nutritious crunch and versatility.

Where can I buy Popkaari? ▼

You can order Popkaari through Amazon, Blinkit for instant delivery, or directly via Razorpay payment gateway. We're expanding to more platforms soon. For any questions, contact us via WhatsApp or email.

What's the shelf life? ▼

Popkaari stays fresh for 3 months from the date of packing when stored in a cool, dry place. Our special packaging ensures maximum freshness and crunch. Once opened, keep in an airtight container.

Do you offer bulk or corporate gifting? ▼

Absolutely! We love corporate partners and custom bulk orders. Reach out to us on WhatsApp at +91-9910657383 for bulk discounts, custom packaging, and corporate gifting solutions.
